FAQs

Discover how HopeMeals connects donors and NGOs to combat food waste and feed those in need.

How does it work?

HopeMeals connects food donors with NGOs through an easy-to-use platform. Donors can upload details and images of food available for donation. NGOs can then claim these donations to distribute meals to those in need.

Who can donate?

Anyone with surplus food can donate through HopeMeals. This includes individuals, restaurants, and grocery stores. Our platform makes it simple to share food and reduce waste.

How do NGOs register?

NGOs can register by filling out a simple form on our website. Once approved, they gain access to the dashboard to claim donations. This process ensures that only verified organizations can distribute food.

Is food safety ensured?

Yes, we prioritize food safety. Our AI verification system checks the freshness of donated food before it's claimed. This ensures that only safe and quality food reaches those in need.
